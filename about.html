About Us

Located in West Lafayette, Indiana, University Place is a continuous care retirement community featuring Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Skilled Nursing Facilities.

A comprehensive description of our facility and services may be found at the web site UniversityPlace.org.

This site is primarily intended for residents of University Place, their family and friends. The site provides information about community activities and links to our monthly newsletters and historical documents.

Use the sidebar at the left or the horizontal navigation bar at the top of this page to explore the features of this site.
